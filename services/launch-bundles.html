FAQ

How do the per-user fees work?

They’re for ongoing administration and support. Microsoft/Google licenses are billed by the vendor. Your total = base bundle price + per-user fee × users + vendor licenses.

What does “setup waived” mean?

We include tenant setup (normally $650–$750) in all Launch Bundles, so you can get secure and organized without an upfront hit.

Can we switch tiers later?

Yes. We’ll adjust the monthly base and per-seat rate at the next billing cycle.
