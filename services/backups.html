Why backups matter for ministry

People trust you with prayer requests, giving records, kids check-in data, and more. Accidental deletion, ransomware, or a failed update shouldn’t put that trust at risk. Solid backups and rehearsed restores make you resilient.

  • Trust & continuity: Recover quickly after accidents or attacks.
  • Compliance & retention: Keep what you must, purge what you should.
  • Board-ready clarity: Clear RPO/RTO targets and monthly reports.
