Why right-sized security matters

Churches and nonprofits handle sensitive information — giving records, counseling notes, kids check-in data. Security should protect people while staying simple enough for staff and volunteers to follow.

  • Trust: A breach breaks more than systems; it breaks confidence.
  • Practicality: Policies and tools that fit your team’s capacity.
  • Resilience: Patching, backups, and response playbooks reduce impact.

At-a-glance

  • MFA & conditional access
  • Endpoint protection & patching
  • Device management (Windows/macOS/iOS/Android)
  • Email hygiene (SPF/DKIM/DMARC, antiphish)
  • Security awareness & phishing drills
  • Incident response & reporting
